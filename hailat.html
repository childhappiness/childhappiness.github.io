Hailat

A family of mother and three children, two girls Fahimah and Hailat, and a boy Ashraf, were abandoned by their father. CHEC has been providing living space for them as they presently cannot afford their own housing. Their mother works as a local washer-woman.

When they first came to CHEC, their entire family has been stricken with what appears to have been yellow fever and jaundice for the better part of a year. Both Ashraf and Fahima had subsequently missed school for almost the entire year.

Ashraf also had an additional undiagnosed infection and found it difficult to sit down for longer than short periods. He was educated at CHEC, which teaches kindergarten to P2, and has now been accepted to another school for P3 for the school year. He is an avid learner and seems very interested in his studies, even at his age. Hailat is currently enrolled at CHEC.
