Child Happiness Education Centre

Child Happiness Education Centre (CHEC) was established in 2011 as a community-based organization (CBO).

Our intention is to make an impact on the growing problem of vulnerable children and out-of-school childrens’ rights to education. CHEC is dedicated to promoting and protecting the rights of children in Uganda with most emphasis on education.

CHEC’s geographical coverage is currently the Kawempe division of Kampala district; however, through child advocacy and community outreach, we may later go beyond Kampala district into nearby Wakiso district to the north.
