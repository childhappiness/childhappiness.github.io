Kato + Waswa

A family of four children (Anderson, Nicholas Waswa, Colline Kato, and Ivan), whose mother, a single parent, brought them to CHEC and promptly disappeared a little less than a year ago. CHEC has now assumed responsibility for the children and provides shelter, food, and clothing for them in addition to their school tuition and scholastic materials.

The middle children Colline and Nicholas are a set of especially outgoing 11 year-old twins, who have been embraced by a group of church-going women from the neighborhood. The twins are always excited to go to church meetings or prayer groups and insist on looking extra neat and smart in their donated clothes before they go. They are outwardly happy and respectlful towards their teachers and other adults.
